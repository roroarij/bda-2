Life Safety Services

The life safety division started decades ago with a couple of Amteck guys doing fire alarm inspections. Today, Life Safety has grown to include dozens of team members across several office locations.

Far from simply performing inspections, the Life Safety team performs fire alarm and suppression system designs and installations, mass notification, monitoring contracts, distributed antenna systems (DAS), and fire extinguisher training.

Mass Notification

Distributed Antenna Systems

A distributed antenna system helps amplify cell phone reception inside buildings where signals are weak. Various DAS setups are available, making it easy for Amteck professionals to find the perfect solution for your business or home. Whether your needs are as simple as preventing dropped calls or fire/safety service access, there are many reasons to choose Amteck.

  • Over 20 years of providing telecommunications and fire/safety service
  • A variety of product lines are available
  • If you’re not sure what you need, we can help! We will happily walk you through a customized approach.

Kentucky's Life Safety Code May Require an In-Building RF Survey

Download RF Survey Info Sheet

In-building safety surveys involve testing the strength of public safety radio frequencies throughout a building. Our specialists will walk through your facility with a floor plan and utilize RF capturing tools to measure signal quality. A digital report is then created, allowing you to know if your life safety systems are in compliance and–if not–where you need to focus your attention.
