Samsara

BLTI has teamed up with Samsara to offer our customers the cutting edge in real-time monitoring, alerts, analytics, and control in a single platform. This provides our customers with a complete and easy-to-use platform that can improve efficiency, safety, and sustainability across your day-to-day operations.  The Samsara line of products can be used across a number of various markets, and help to improve your quality of reporting, and most of all save your company time and money.  

 

Samsara for Water & Waste Water

Samsara is an Industrial IoT company that provides water companies with end-to-end data visibility, analytics, and control from an industrial gateway with built-in LTE connectivity, wireless vibration sensors, and the Samsara cloud dashboard— all in one integrated, easy-to-use, affordable platform. Customers trust Samsara’s technology to drive business outcomes across remote sites and in primary operating facilities: to optimize performance, drive efficiency, prevent downtime, save time and ensure safety for site workers, and to deliver superior service to their customers.

Samsara for Oil & Gas

Employ Samsara's remote and control monitoring solutions into your operations which will deliver unparalleled value for industrial customers. The remote monitoring and control solution allows you to obtain complete visibility into issues via real-time monitoring, alerts, analytics, reporting, and control on a single platform. This will enable you to maximize your efficiency by providing the tools for you to identify, diagnose, and resolve issues in real-time which will minimize downtime, and increase your profitability.
