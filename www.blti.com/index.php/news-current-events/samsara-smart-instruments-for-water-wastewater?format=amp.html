Samsara Smart Instruments for Water & Wastewater

Samsara is an easy-to-use platform designed to fully improve efficiency, safety and sustainability across all your operations of wastewater! ​

What can Samsara do for you?

  • Easily connected tanks, pumps wells, lift stations, and existing SCADA systems and even start using data in real-time across the entire organization
  • Monitor and alert level, pressure, flow, vibration, and temperature to identify issues, prevent any or all downtime and effectively schedule maintenance for you
  • Supports operators with mobile access to monitor dashboards, alert notifications, and even has the ability to control and manage setpoints remotely

BLTI is proud to utilize Samsara as it is designed to provide water companies with accurate visibility, analytics, and control for the wireless user. Samsara also offers a cloud dashboard- providing an all-in-one integrated, easy-to-use platform. If you are looking to drive your business outcomes and become a remote site for your operating facilities, look no further than BLTI where we can offer a state-of-the-art platform.

Samsara for Water & Wastewater - One Page Overview

The two features of an Industrial Gateway and the Cloud Dashboard combined, making the platform for BLTI seamless. The Industrial gateway allows you to remotely save data directly into the cloud dashboard and be able to visualize your data. There are user-friendly drag-and-drop tools to make it easy for all to build the dashboard of your choice!
