How Easy is it to Add Video Management to my Security System?

Feb 25 Posted by Candace in BLTI

Every business is unique. A one-size-fits-all solution no longer works for modern security needs. This is where the beauty of integration arises. BLTI can create partnerships with differing solutions, where teamwork can make your dream work. Connecting like puzzle pieces, the ability of a video management system to integrate with your current system is essential not only to your solution but to your budget.

An example of this is Gallagher's Command Centre integration solutions. It is designed to integrate seamlessly with a wide variety of trusted video management systems and hardware to ensure that your system runs smoothly and safely while increasing efficiencies and reducing costs.

Gallagher's Video Viewer Software Development Kit provides partnering video management systems with a complete set of tools to integrate easily and effectively. This gives you the ability to easily customize your system to your security needs. A few of its featured partners include Salient Systems' CompleteView and Bosch's Video Management System.

The combined solution of Gallagher's Command Centre and Salient's CompleteView automatically associate access control and video incidents, providing search and reporting capabilities through both product interfaces.

Here is a list of some of the features available through this partnership:

Record an event from triggers and respond to video motion detection alarms with live camera view on-demand with CompleteView Spotlight View

  • Recorded video is automatically associated to events
  • Video playback for alarms and events
  • CompleteView provides capabilities such as logical camera grouping, email or text alerts, flexible FPS settings, and web browser support
  • CompleteView is fully scalable to fit your business


Gallagher's partnership with Bosch's Video Management System also provides video management capabilities, including:

  • Live viewing and stored footage, whether you are actively responding to an incident or needing to review a past incident
  • Receive motion and diagnostic alarms, providing a single audit trail for all alarms and allowing operators to monitor and manage incidents
  • Receive playback video for incidents
  • Bosch cameras can be preset based on alarms
  • Gallagher Command Centre works directly with Bosch's Video Management System to view live video feeds and provide pan-tilt-zoom control for cameras
