BLTI is Hiring!

In Today's job market, jobs are ever more evolving and changing, just as the supply chain demand has come around. It is so important when choosing a career, job or lifestyle, to understand what your values and needs are. As an individual, what is it you're looking for? A remote job, a 9-5 job, a fast pace environment, or a slow one? There are so many factors to consider when looking into a career. BLTI offers a variety of these types of jobs.

If you are looking for a fast pace, office environment then you might be a great fit for some of the following jobs:

  • Control System Specialist
  • Project Manager – Fire Alarm and Security
  • Sr. Project Engineer
  • Operations Support Specialist
  • Technical Sales Representative

These careers offer a variety of options, allowing for certain positions to get out of the office and into the field, but mostly in office environment with a hard-working team!

If getting your hands dirty, working out in the field and attention to detail is your specialty, then we also have the job for you! We offer a variety of positions for our Field Technicians, some out on job sites and others in our shop. These positions offer an earlier start, but an earlier finish! If this appeals to you, check out the positions below:

  • Security/Fire Field Technician
  • Sr. Field Service Technician
  • Warehouse/Shop Assistant
  • Electrical Assembly Technician II

Lastly, if you find yourself with more experience, and wanting a managerial role, BLTI has a variety of positions, relating to sales, technicians and engineering!

  • Field Service Manager
  • Sr. Project Engineering (Instrumentation and Controls)
  • Technical Sales Representative

If you are looking for a company that offers a variety of full-time benefits, IRA, PTO, Life Insurance, Paid Holiday's and a variety of hours for your work week, BLTI is the company for you. Our team is hard working, dedicated and ready to tackle any task at hand. Contact HR today if interested or check out our website and apply today!
