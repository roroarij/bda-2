BLTI Awarded Contract with BuyBoard to Offer Security, Access Control, and Fire Alarm Products and Services

Nov 15 Posted by Candace in BLTI

11/15/2021

TOMBALL, TX — BL Technology, Inc. (BLTI) was recently awarded a contract with the National Purchasing Cooperative, BuyBoard for Fire, Security Systems, and Monitoring Services under contract number 654-21. Starting on 1 December 2021 BLTI will be able to provide a wide array of security and life safety products including monitoring services.

BLTI has been approved for access control, intrusion alarms, fire alarms, and more. Please contact us if you are interested in any of these products below.

  • CyberLock – key centric access control solution
  • DSX – access control and system monitoring
  • Gallagher – Intruder Alarms, Perimeter Security, and Access Control
  • Honeywell Farenhyt & Silent Knight – Intelligent Fire Systems
  • Intrusion and fire alarm monitoring
  • Installation, Repair, and general service of security and fire alarm systems

BuyBoard was originally created by the Texas Association of School Boards in 1998 to increase the purchasing power and simplify the process by using a customized online purchasing system. Developed to comply with state laws that require governmental entities to make purchases from an approved list of vendors who have gone through a competitive procurement process, BuyBoard gives members the advantage of leveraging the Cooperative's ability to obtain bulk discounts, combined with the ease of online, web-based shopping.
