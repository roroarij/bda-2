About Us

A&A Alarm Systems began when Jerry Bourque opened the company 30 years ago working from his home in Seffner. The rapid commercial and residential growth in the region, combined with the increased need for fire and security alarm systems in existing properties, helped the business to quickly grow; so Jerry brought both his sons, Peter and Greg, into the business to work alongside him. Today, Peter Bourque, President, manages company operations, Greg Bourque, Vice President, manages ongoing projects and service, while Jerry, VP Sales, develops new business. Recently, Buddy Bennett joined the company as CEO to streamline the finance and administrative operations.

We are respected members of numerous associations

Our Mission

Provide peace of mind for our clients through the fire and security products we sell, certify, and service.
