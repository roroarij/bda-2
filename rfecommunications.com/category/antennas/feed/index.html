These units are perfect for large-scale office facilities that require a boost in communication and cell phone signal for all carriers, including T-Mobile and Verizon.

The system is made up of several components, starting with the exterior antenna that sources the signal from local cell towers. From there, the signal is sent to the amplifiers and then out to the passive components via coax. The passive components allow for multiple antennas to be placed throughout the system. Finally, the actual broadcast antenna is used to transmit the signal to its intended source.
