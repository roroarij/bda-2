RFE Communications travels nationwide installing systems built to connect people to first responders. Keeping everyone connected is our goal! In times of emergency, you want to be connected and STAY connected. While we connect you to first responders, we also keep first responders connected to each other with ERCES solutions. This ensures all lines of communication are clear and connected. Our technicians work across the nation, RFE can and will come to you. We are available to service you and your team to provide a connectivity solution anywhere.

Our project managers, project estimators and project coordinators are your liaisons in this whole process from start to finish and will communicate with you until the end! We boost your cellular connectivity and first responder radio connectivity through our professionally designed RFE Communications systems. 

RFE Communications is a distribution company, engineering firm and installation partner that can service all your project needs regarding Emergency Responder Radio Coverage System or Cellular Distributed Antenna Systems.
